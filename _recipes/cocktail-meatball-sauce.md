## Ingredients

- 1/3 C. green pepper
- 2 T brown sugar
- 1/3 C. onion
- 4 T Worcestershire sauce
- 2 T butter
- 1 T mustard
- 1 can tomato soup
- 1 T vinegar

## Preparation

Saut√© first 3 ingredients, just until tender.  Mix next 5 ingredients and add to vegetables.  Pour over cooked meatballs and put in crock pot on high for 1 hour.  Turn on low to keep warm and serve.
