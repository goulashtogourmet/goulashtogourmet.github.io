## Ingredients

- 3 T orange juice (or milk)
- 1 tsp. curry powder
- 3 T butter, melted
- 1 tsp. salt
- 1/4 C. mustard
- 3 lb. chicken pcs.
- 1/2 C. honey

## Preparation

Mix first 6 ingredients together in sauce pan.  Pour over chicken pieces in a shallow roasting pan.Bake at 350 degrees for about 1 hr., basting often.
