## Ingredients

- 1 square chocolate
- 1 1/2 C.  powdered sugar
- 2 T. shortening
- 1 tsp. vanilla
- 5 T. milk

## Preparation

Heat chocolate and shortening, add milk and stir in powdered sugar. Spread on brownies.
