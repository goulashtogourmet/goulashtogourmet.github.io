## Ingredients

- 1 lb. ground beef
- 1 can chicken gumbo soup
- 1/2 C. onion
- 1 tsp. brown sugar
- 3 T catsup
- 2 T mustard

## Preparation

Brown beef and onion together in pan.  Add rest of ingredients and cook until everything is well blended.  Serve on buns.
