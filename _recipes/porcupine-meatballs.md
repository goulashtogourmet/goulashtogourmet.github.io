## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 lbs. ground beef
- 1/4 C.  onion, chopped
- 1/2 C. rice
- 1 egg
- 1 tsp. salt
- 1 can tomato soup
- 1/4 tsp. pepper
- 1/2 C. water

## Preparation

Mix first 6 ingredients and form meatballs.  Brown in Dutch oven.  Mix soup and water, pour over meatballs.  Cover and simmer for 45 to 60 min.
