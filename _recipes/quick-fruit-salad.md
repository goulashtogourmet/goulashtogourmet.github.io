## Ingredients

- 1 can pineapple chunks, drained
- 1 C. green grapes
- 1 can peach pie filling
- 1 C. sliced bananas
- 1 can mandarin oranges, drained

## Preparation

Combine all, except bananas, and chill until ready to serve.  Add bananas just before serving.
