## Ingredients

- 2 T butter
- 2 cans cream of mushroom soup
- 1/2 C. chopped onion
- 1 can bean sprouts
- 2 C. chopped celery
- 1 pkg. chow mein noodles
- 2 C. chicken, cut coarse
- 1 small can cashews

## Preparation

Put noodles on bottom of 9 x 13 dish, then rest of ingredients.  Bake at 350 degrees until bubbles.
