## Ingredients

- 16 oz. cooked, tail-on shrimp, thawed
- 1/4 C. dry white wine
- 2 T butter or margarine
- 1 T Dijon mustard
- 1 1/2 C. sliced fresh mushrooms
- 1 tsp. crushed dried tarragon
- 1/4 C. finely chopped onion
- 1/4 tsp. paprika
- 1 T flour
- 1/4 tsp. pepper
- 3/4 C. chicken broth
- 1/4 heavy cream

## Preparation

In large skillet, melt butter.  Saut√© mushrooms and onion until limp.  Stir in flour until mixed; add chicken broth and wine, stirring.  Stir in mustard and seasonings.  Cook until mixture thickens and bubbles.  Reduce heat and stir in cream and shrimp.  Cover and cook 2 min. more or until heated through. Serve over rice.
