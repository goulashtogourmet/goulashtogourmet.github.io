## Ingredients

- 8 oz. med. noodles
- 1 C. sour cream
- 1 tsp. salt
- 2 C. cooked chicken
- 3 C. water
- 2 C. cubed ham
- 1 C. chopped onion
- 1 C. stuffed olive halves
- 2 T. butter
- 1 4 oz. jar pimento
- 1 green pepper
- 1 14 oz. can artichokes (not oiled)
- 1 can cream of mushroom soup
- 1/2 C. crushed crackers (mixed w/ 2 T. melted butter)

## Preparation

Cook and drain noodles, salt and water.  Saut√© chopped onion, butter and green pepper for one minute.  Blend all ingredients in a 3 qt. casserole.  Top with bread crumbs.  Bake at 325 degrees for one hour.  Parmesan cheese may be served on top.
