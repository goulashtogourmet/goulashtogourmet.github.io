## Ingredients

- 1 C. margarine
- 1 tsp. soda
- 1 C. brown sugar
- 1 tsp. salt
- 1 C. white sugar
- 1 tsp. cream of tartar
- 1 C. oil
- 1 C. oatmeal
- 1 egg
- 1 C. coconut
- 2 tsp vanilla
- 1 C. Rice Krispies
- 3 1/2 C. flour
- 1 12 oz. pkg. chocolate chips

## Preparation

Mix all ingredients together thoroughly.  Drop onto greased cookie sheet.  Bake 10 to 12 minutes at 375 degrees.
