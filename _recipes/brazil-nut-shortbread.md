## Ingredients

- 1 C. butter
- 2 C. flour
- 1/2 C. sugar
- 1 C. sliced Brazil nuts

## Preparation

Cream butter and sugar.  Work flour into cream mixture.  Blend in nuts.  Flatten bits of dough on ungreased cookie sheet.  Bake 15 to 20 minutes at 300 degrees.
