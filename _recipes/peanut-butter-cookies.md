## Ingredients

- 1/4 C. butter
- 1/2 C. peanut butter
- 1/2 C. brown sugar
- 1 egg
- 1/2 C. white sugar
- 1 C. flour mixed with 1 tsp soda

## Preparation

Cream first 3 ingredients.  Stir in peanut butter and egg.  Add flour and soda mixture.  Form in balls and press down with fork.  Bake 12 minutes at 350 degrees.
