## Ingredients

- 8-10 marshmallows
- 1/3 C. brown sugar
- 1/3 C. chopped nuts

## Preparation

Cut 8 or 10 marshmallows in half and arrange over cake batter.  Sprinkle 1/3 C.  brown sugar and 1/3 C.  chopped nuts on top and bake 45 minutes at 350 degrees.  Serve warm.
