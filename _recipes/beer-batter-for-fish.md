## Ingredients

- 1C. flour
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp. baking powder
- 1 C. beer
- 1 egg

## Preparation

Mix all ingredients together.  Dip fish fillets in batter.  Let each fillet drip over bowl for a few seconds, then fry in a skillet with vegetable oil.
