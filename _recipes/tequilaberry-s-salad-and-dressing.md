## Ingredients

- SALAD:
- 1 head lettuce
- 2 lbs bacon, fried crisp and crumbled
- 1 head cauliflower
- 1 C. Parmesan cheese
- DRESSING:
- 2 C. mayo (Greek yogurt may be substituted.)
- 1 C. sugar
- 1 C. buttermilk

## Preparation

Combine salad ingredients.  Just before serving, add combined dressing ingredients and toss.
