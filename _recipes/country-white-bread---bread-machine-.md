## Ingredients

- 1 pkg. (1/4 oz) active dry yeast
- 1 egg, beaten
- 1 C. warm water (110 to 115 degrees)
- 2 T vegetable oil
- 1/4 C. sugar
- 4 C. all purpose flour
- 1 1/2 tsp salt

## Preparation

Follow instructions for your bread machine.
