## Ingredients

- 1 lb. fresh asparagus
- 1/2 to 1 C. chicken broth
- Pinch of sugar
- 1/4 C. butter, melted

## Preparation

Clean asparagus and cut into 2 inch pieces.  Bring chicken broth to a boil, add asparagus and sugar.  Steam, covered, until tender.  Drizzle with melted butter.
