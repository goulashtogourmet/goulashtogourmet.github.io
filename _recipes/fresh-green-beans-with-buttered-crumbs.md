## Ingredients

- Fresh whole green beans
- 1/2 stick of butter (plus 1 T.)
- 1 C. crushed soda crackers
- Dash of salt

## Preparation

Cook whole green beans in salted water until firm, but tender.  Drain and add 1 T butter.  While beans are cooking, sautÃ© 1 C. finely crushed cracker crumbs in 1/2 stick melted butter.  Stir constantly, until light brown and crispy.  Top beans with buttered crumbs and serve.
