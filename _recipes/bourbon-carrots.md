## Ingredients

- 5 T butter
- 2 T bourbon
- 5 med. carrots cut in thin slices
- 1 tsp. (or more) light brown sugar
- 1 med. onion thinly sliced
- Salt

## Preparation

Melt butter, add carrots and onions, cook 5 min.  Mix in bourbon, sugar and salt.  Cover and cook until tender.
