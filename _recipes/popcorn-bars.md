## Ingredients

- 1 C. sugar
- 1 C. peanut butter
- 1 C. corn syrup
- 1 C. salted peanuts
- 6 C. + popcorn

## Preparation

Cook sugar and syrup over medium heat until mixture begins to boil; remove from heat.  Add 1 C. peanut butter, mix well.  Add popcorn and nuts, mix well.  Press into buttered 9 x 13 pan.
