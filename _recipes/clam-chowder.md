## Ingredients

- 1 T garlic
- 2 cans chicken broth
- 1 small onion
- 1 can clams
- Diced carrots
- 2 cans cream of potato soup
- Diced potatoes
- Pepper to taste
- 2 T melted butter
- ½ C. half and half

## Preparation

Sauté first 5 ingredients.  Add next 3 ingredients, bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer 15 minutes.  Add last 2 ingredients and heat thoroughly.
