## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 3 C. flour
- 1 C. salad oil
- 1 T cinnamon
- 2 C. sugar
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1 T vanilla
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 C. raw grated zucchini
- 1 C. chopped walnuts

## Preparation

Beat eggs until foamy; add sugar, oil, vanilla and zucchini.  Mix well, but lightly.  Add dry ingredients and blend.  Stir in nuts.  Bake in a 9 x 13 pain for 1 hour at 350 degrees.
